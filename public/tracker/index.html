How this is built. The tracker is compiled by hand from national statistical office releases (ONS UK, Insee France, Destatis Germany, Korea KOSIS, Japan e-Stat, Istat Italy, INE Spain, US CDC Wonder, etc.). YoY deltas use comparable prior-period releases where available. Figures are provisional unless marked final by the issuing agency. Last refresh date reflects the most recent national release ingested, not a Demoria publication date.
